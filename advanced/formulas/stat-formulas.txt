HITTING STATS:

OBP (on base percentage):
OBP = (Hits + Walks + Hit by Pitch) / (At Bats + Walks + Hit by Pitch + Sacrifice Flies)

SLG (slugging average):
Total bases (including extra base-hits) divided by at-bats
SLG = ((1*singles) + (2*doubles) + (3*triples) + (4*HRs)) / AB
or 
TB (total bases) divided by at bats

OPS (on-base slugging):
Considered to be an overall assessment of a players production.
OPS = OBP + SLG


PITCHING STATS:

ERA (earned run average)
(ER / IP) * 9
